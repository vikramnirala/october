Did you know

Over 93% of people who have a Medicare Part D prescription drug plan are not sure if it's the best one available to them... or even what medications it will cover if they need them.

Many seniors just buy the cheapest thing, not knowing that it may have huge deductibles or bureaucracy involved if they need medications later.

The sources of confusion Medicare Patients have with Medicare Part D Plans are endless.... but here are the end results.

  • They pay way too much for the plan
  • The plan does not cover the medications they need
  • Even if it does cover your medication, it may require laborious processes (that are difficult to understand and follow) to get the particular medicine your doctor prescribes.
  • When sick, they have to fight with the insurance company to get help... at the time when you are least able to.
  • Once you realize you have the wrong plan, they make you wait for up to a year before you can find the right plan for you.
  • Even then, the insurance companies can change their coverages including the medications they cover in the middle of the year,.... after you are already "locked in."
  • Many suffer permanent health consequences and even premature death because they didn't get the care they needed... even when it would have been available had they done the right things.
